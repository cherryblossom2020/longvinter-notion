Longvinter’s In-game Regulations
We want to ensure that Longvinter provides a good environment that is both healthy and positive for all players, as it should. To accomplish this, we would like to implement these in-game regulations. The following content includes some of the actions and behaviours that will result in your being banned from Longvinter.
Bad Conduct
If you engage in disruptive, abusive, or discriminatory behaviours, you will be punished according to the severity of your actions. The following are a few examples of things you should avoid:
Offensive language, insulting, harassing, or threatening
Discrimination or hate speech that is based on, including but not limited to, sexual orientation, gender identity, race, religion, and ethnicity.
Inappropriate game names, such as lewd, derogatory, or insulting.
Cheating / Exploits
It is unacceptable to use cheats, hacks, exploits, or bugs to gain an unfair advantage over other players, ruining their gaming experience. It will result in a permanent ban.
Penalties and Bans
The duration of penalties will be applied based on the severity of the offence. More serious offences will result in a longer suspension and it also depends on your behaviour history.
